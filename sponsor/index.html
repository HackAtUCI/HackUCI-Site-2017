Recap
HackUCI 2017 was the most successful hackathon we have ever hosted. Held on January 13-15, 2017 at the University of California – Irvine, over 400 hackers participated and put together their time, energy and skills over 36 hours to turn ideas into reality. For HackUCI 2018, we plan to bring a more diverse and talented array of hackers and build the most innovative technology of tomorrow!
